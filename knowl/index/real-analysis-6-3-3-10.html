Definition 11.1.3.

Let \(V\) be a normed vector space. For \(\varphi \in V^*\) define \(\|\varphi\|_{\mathrm{op}}\) by
🔗
\begin{equation*} \|\varphi\|_{\mathrm{op}} = \sup\{|\varphi(v)| \colon \|v\|=1\}. \end{equation*}
We call \(\|\cdot\|_{\mathrm{op}}\) the operator norm on \(V^*\text{.}\)
