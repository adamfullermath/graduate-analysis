If \(\{(X_k,\M_k,\mu_k)\}_{k=1}^n\) are a collection of $n$ measure spaces, we define the measurable rectangles in \(X = \prod_{k=1}^n X_k\) as the sets
\begin{equation*} \left\{\prod_{k=1}^N A_k \colon A_k \in \mathcal{M}_k\, \text{for }k=1,\ldots,n \right\}. \end{equation*}
We can similarly define a product measure on \(X\text{.}\) We leave the details of the next proposition to the exercises, Exercise 1.4.6 and Exercise 1.4.7.
