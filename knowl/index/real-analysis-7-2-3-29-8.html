Definition 1.1.17.

Let \(m\) be the Lebesgue measure on the Lebesgue measurable subsets of \(\mathbb{R}\) and let \(n\geq 1\text{.}\) Let \(m_n\) be the product measure
🔗
\begin{equation*} m_n = \underbrace{m \times \ldots \times m}_{n\ \text{times}} \end{equation*}
on \(\mathbb{R}^n\text{.}\) We call \(m_n\) the Lebesgue measure on \(\mathbb{R}^n\). The \(\sigma\)-algebra of subsets which are measurable with respect to \(m_n\) are the Lebesgue measurable subsets of \(\mathbb{R}^n\).
