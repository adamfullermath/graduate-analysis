Theorem 1.2.8. Regularity of Lebesgue measure on \(\bR^n\).

Let \(E \subseteq \bR^n\) be a Lebesgue measurable set. Then
\begin{equation*} m_n(E) = \inf\{m_n(U) \colon E \subseteq U,\ U\text{ open}\}, \end{equation*}
and
\begin{equation*} m_n(E) = \inf\{m_n(K) \colon K \subseteq E,\ K \text{ compact}\}. \end{equation*}
