Definition 2.3.3.

Let \(X\) be a non-empty set, and let \(\mathcal{S} \subseteq \mathcal{P}(X)\) be a non-empty collection of subsets. Let \(\mu \colon \mathcal{S} \rightarrow [0,\infty]\) be a function and let \(\mu^*\) be the outer measure constructed in Theorem 2.3.2. We call \(\mu^*\) the outer measure induced by \(\mu\).
