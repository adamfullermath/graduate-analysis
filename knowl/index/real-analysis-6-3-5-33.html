Definition 2.3.10.

Let \(X\) be a locally compact Hausdorff space, let \(K \subseteq X\) be a compact subset, and let \(\{U_1,\ldots,U_n\}\) be an open cover for \(K\text{.}\) A collection of continuous functions \(h_1,\ldots,h_n\) from \(X\) to \([0,1]\) are partition of unity for \(K\) subordinate to \(U_1,\ldots,U_n\) if
🔗
  1. \(h_i \in C_c(X)\) for \(1\leq i \leq n\text{;}\)
    🔗
    🔗
  2. \(\sum_{i=1}^n h_i(x) = 1\) for all \(x \in K\text{;}\) and
    🔗
    🔗
  3. the compact support of \(h_i\) is a subset of \(U_i\) for \(1\leq i \leq n\text{.}\)

    🔗
