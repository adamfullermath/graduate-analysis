Lemma 2.3.13.

Let \(\mathcal{S}\) be a semiring of subsets of a non-empty subset \(X\) and let
\begin{equation*} \mathcal{S}' = \left\{ \bigcup_{k=1}^n C_k : \{C_k\}_{k=1}^n \text{ finite disjoint collection in }\mathcal{S}\right\}. \end{equation*}
Then \(\mathcal{S}'\) is closed under relative complements. Further, if \(\mu\) is a premeasure on \(\mathcal{S}\) then \(\mu\) extends uniquely to a premeasure on \(\mathcal{S}'\text{.}\)
