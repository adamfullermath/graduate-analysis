Exercise 3.

Let \(X\) be a non-empty set, and let \(c\) be the counting measure on the \(\sigma\)-algebra \(P(X)\) of all subsets of \(X\text{.}\) We will show that \((\ell^1(X))^* \simeq \ell^\infty(X)\) by the same isomorphism used in Theorem 2.2.4.
🔗
  1. For each \(f \in \ell^1(X)\text{,}\) let \(L_f = \{x \in X \colon f(x) \neq 0\}\text{.}\) Show that \(L_f\) is a countable set, and that
    \begin{equation*} \int_X f\,dc = \sum_{x \in L_f} f(x). \end{equation*}
    🔗
    🔗
  2. Take \(g \in \ell^\infty(X)\text{.}\) Define
    \begin{equation*} T_g(f) = \int_X gf\,dc \end{equation*}
    for all \(f \in \ell^1(X)\text{.}\) Show that \(T_g\) is a linear functional on \(\ell^1(X)\) with \(\|T_g\|_\mathrm{op}= \|g\|_\infty\text{.}\) (You should be able to prove this directly without reference to the proof of Lemma 2.2.2).
    🔗
    🔗
    🔗
  3. Take \(\varphi\in (\ell^1(X))^*\text{.}\) For each \(x \in X\text{,}\) let \(e_x\) be the function
    \begin{equation*} e_x(y) = \begin{cases} 1 &\text{if }y=x\\ 0 & \text{if }y\neq x.\end{cases} \end{equation*}
    Show that \(e_x \in \ell^1(X)\) with \(\|e_x\|_1 = 1\text{,}\) for all \(x\in X\text{.}\)
    🔗
    Define a function \(g\) on \(X\) by \(g(x) = \varphi(e_x)\text{.}\) Show that \(g\in \ell^\infty(X)\text{.}\) Show that \(\varphi = T_g\text{.}\)
    🔗
    🔗
