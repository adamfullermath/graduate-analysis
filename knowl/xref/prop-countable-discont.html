Proposition 7.4.11.

Let \([a,b]\subseteq \mathbb{R}\) be a closed interval, and let \(A \subseteq [a,b]\) be a countable set. There is an increasing function \(f\) on \([a,b]\) where \(f\) is continuous on \([a,b]\backslash A\) and discontinuous on \(A\text{.}\) Further, \(f\) can be chosen to be right-continuous.
