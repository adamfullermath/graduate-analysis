Proposition 1.1.16.

Let \((X_1,\mathcal{M}_1,\mu_1)\text{,}\) \((X_2,\mathcal{M}_2,\mu_2)\text{,}\) and \((X_3,\mathcal{M}_3,\mu_3)\) be \(\sigma\)-finite measure spaces. Then
\begin{equation*} \mu_1 \times \mu_2 \times \mu_3 =(\mu_1 \times \mu_2) \times \mu_3 =\mu_1 \times (\mu_2 \times \mu_3). \end{equation*}
