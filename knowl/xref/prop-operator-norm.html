Proposition 2.1.4.

Let \(V\) be a normed vector space and let \(\|\cdot\|_{\mathrm{op}}\) be the operator norm on the dual \(V^*\text{.}\) Then \(\|\cdot\|_{\mathrm{op}}\) is a norm on \(V^*\text{.}\)
🔗
Further
🔗
\begin{equation*} \|\varphi\|_{\mathrm{op}} = \inf \{M \colon |\varphi(v)|\leq M\|v\| \text{ for all }v \in V\}, \end{equation*}
for all \(\varphi \in V^*.\)
