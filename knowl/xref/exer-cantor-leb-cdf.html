Exercise 11.

Let \(C \subseteq [0,1]\) be the Cantor set; let \(U = [0,1]\backslash C\text{;}\) and let \(\varphi \colon [0,1] \rightarrow [0,1]\text{.}\) Let \(\mu\) be the Borel measure on \([0,1]\) induced by \(\varphi\) (using Theorem 3.3.7). Show that \(\mu([0,1]) = 1\text{;}\) \(\mu(C) = 1\) and \(\mu(U) = 0\text{.}\)
