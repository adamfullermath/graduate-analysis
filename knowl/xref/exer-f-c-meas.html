Exercise 1.

Let \((X,\mathcal{F})\) be a measurable space, and let \(f\) be a measurable extended real-valued function with measurable domain \(E\text{.}\) Show that the set
🔗
\begin{equation*} \{x \in E \colon f(x) =c \} \end{equation*}
is measurable for all \(c \in \mathbb{R}\cup \{-\infty,\infty\}.\) (HINT: you will need to treat the values \(c=-\infty\) and \(c=\infty\) differently than \(c \in \mathbb{R}\)).
