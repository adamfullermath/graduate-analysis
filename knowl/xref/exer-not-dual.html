Exercise 4.

Let \(X\) be an uncountable set, and let \(c\) be the counting measure on the \(\sigma\)-algebra \(P(X)\) of all subsets of \(X\text{.}\) Let \(c_0\) be the restriction of \(c\) to the \(\sigma\)-algebra \(\mathcal{M}\) of all subsets of \(X\) which are either countable or cocountable (see Exercise II.2.4.1 for more on \(\mathcal{M}\text{.}\)) We will show that \((L^1(X,c_0))^*\) is not \(L^\infty(X,c_0)\text{.}\) Exercise 2.5.3 should be completed before this exercise.
