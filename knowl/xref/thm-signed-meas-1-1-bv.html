Theorem 4.2.14.

Let \(\mu\) be a signed Borel measure on \([a,b]\) be a signed Borel measure on \([a,b]\text{.}\) Then \(f(x) = \nu([a,x])\) is a right-continuous function of bounded variation.
🔗
Conversely, if \(f\) is a right-continuous function of bounded variation on \([a,b]\text{,}\) then there is a unique Borel signed measure \(\mu\) on \([a,b]\) such that \(\mu([a,x]) = f(x)\) for all \(x \in [a,b]\text{.}\)
