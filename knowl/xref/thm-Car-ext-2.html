Theorem 2.3.16. Carathéodory-Hahn Theorem.

Let \(\mu_0\) be a premeasure on a semiring \(\mathcal{S}\) of subsets of a non-empty set \(X\text{.}\) Then the Carathéodory measure \(\mu\) induced by \(\mu_0\) is an extension of \(\mu_0\text{.}\) Furthermore, if \(\mu_0\) is \(\sigma\)-finite, then \(\mu\) is \(\sigma\)-finite and \(\mu\) is the unique measure on the set of \(\mu^*\)-measurable subsets extending \(\mu_0\text{.}\)
