Checkpoint 2.4.11.

Prove the claim in Example Example 2.3.5: Let \(\mathcal{S}\) be the collection of all open intervals in the real line. Let \(\mu\) be the length function on \(\mathcal{S}\text{,}\) i.e. \(\mu(I) = \ell(I)\) for all \(I \in \mathcal{S}\text{.}\) Let \(\mu^*\) be the outer measure induced by \(\mu\text{.}\) Show that \(\mu^*(I) = \ell(I)\) for all intervals \(I\) in \(\mathbb{R}\text{.}\)
