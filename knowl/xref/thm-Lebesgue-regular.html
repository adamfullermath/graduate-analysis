Theorem 5.1.8.

Let \(E \subseteq \mathbb{R}\) be Lebesgue measurable, then
🔗
  1. (outer regularity)
    \begin{equation*} m^*(E) = \inf \{ m(U) \colon E \subseteq U,\ U \text{ open}\}; \end{equation*}
    🔗
    🔗
  2. (inner regularity)
    \begin{equation*} m^*(E) = \sup \{ m(K) \colon K \subseteq E,\ K \text{ compact}\}. \end{equation*}
    🔗
    🔗
