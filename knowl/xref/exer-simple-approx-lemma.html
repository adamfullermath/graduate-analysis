Exercise 10.

Let \((X,\mathcal{M},\mu)\) be a measure space and let \(f \colon X \rightarrow \mathbb{R}\) be a bounded measurable function. Show that, for all \(\varepsilon > 0\) there exist simple functions \(\varphi\) and \(\psi\) such that \(\varphi \leq f \leq \psi\) and \(0 \leq \psi - \varphi < \varepsilon\) on \(X\text{.}\)
