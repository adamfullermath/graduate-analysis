Lemma 2.2.1.

Let \((X,\mathcal{M},\mu)\) be a measure space, fix \(p\) with \(1< p<\infty\) and let \(q\) be conjugate to \(p\text{.}\) The map
🔗
\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} T \colon & L^q(X,\mu) \rightarrow (L^p(X,\mu))^* \\ & g \mapsto T_g \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
is a linear isometric map (i.e. \(\|T_g\|_{\mathrm{op}} = \|g\|_q\) for all \(g \in L^q(X,\mu)\)).
