Lemma 2.2.8.

Let \(G\) be a locally compact Hausdorff group and let \(\mu\) be a Radon measure on \(G\text{.}\) Define a Radon measure \(\tilde{\mu}\) by
\begin{equation*} \tilde{\mu}(A) = \mu(A^{-1}). \end{equation*}
If \(\mu\) is a left Haar measure, then \(\tilde{\mu}\) is a right Haar measure; if \(\mu\) is a right Haar measure, then \(\tilde{\mu}\) is a left Haar measure.
