Exercise 10.

Let \(\|\cdot\|_\mathrm{op}\) be the norm defined on \(M_{n\times n}(\bR)\text{,}\) the \(n\times n\) matrices, defined in Remark 1.3.2. For each \(1 \leq p \leq \infty\text{,}\) define the \(p\)-norm on \(M_{n\times n}(\bR)\) by identifying \(M_{n\times n}(\bR)\) with \(\bR^{n\times n}\text{.}\) Show that \(\|\cdot\|_\mathrm{op}\) is equivalent to \(\|\cdot\|_p\) for \(1 \leq p \leq \infty.\)
